“Lin2 kiam2 bo5 kong2, iau2 u7 si3 goeh8 jit8, chiu7 siu-koah e5 si5 kau3 mah? Goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2, kiah8-bak8 khoaN3 hng5 i2-keng sek8, thang koah lah.” 
Goa2 na7 mng7 hia e5 hiaN-ti7 kong2, “Lin2 u7 siuN7 beh koh tng2-lai5 Sai-pou7 a2-bo5?”  In kong2, “SiuN7 si7 oe7 siuN7, chong2-si7 au7-soaN thou5 chin liam5”, i3-su3 si7 kiaN5 boe7 khui-kha, in-ui7 ho2 than3-chiah8, thai2 u7-iaN2 au7-soaN e5 thou5 u7 liam5 leh?
Kau3 hit e7-am3, ou-am3-mi5 pek-oa2, si3-ui5 long2 an-cheng7 e5 si5, Ma2-li7 e5 sim chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 kam2-kak kou-toaN put-an, kiaN-hiaN5.  In-ui7 i long2 m7-bat chit8-e5 ti7 chit-khoan2 am3-sam3 e5 sou2-chai7 ke3-me5.  Sou2-i2 Ma2-li7 chi2-u7 pho7 kau2-a2 ti7 i e5 sin-piN m7-kaN2 khun3, lai5 teh tiuN-ti5. 
Ma2-li7 u7-si5 tioh8 eng7 siang-chhiu2 lai5 am i e5 bak8-chiu, u7-si5 that-bat8 i e5 hi7-khang, kiaN-liau2 khoaN3-tioh8 sim2-mih8, thiaN-tioh8 sim2-mih8.  Sit8-chai7 Ma2-li7 soe3-soe3 e5 sim long2 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang tam-tng chit-chiong2 e5 kiaN-hiaN5.
U7 chit8-jit8 goa2 ti7 koe-lou7 teh kiaN5 u7 tu2-tioh8 chit8-e5 soe3-han3 gin2-na2 hiong-hiong chau2 chhoan3 ke3--khi3. Hit si5 loh8 seh tu2-a2 chiN5, koe-lou7 chhi5- chhi5. Hit-e5 gin2-na2 bo5 soe3-ji7, teh chau2 e5 si5 kha tioh8-tak poah8-to2.
Ke3 kui2 jit8 koh tit-tioh8 ki-hoe7 thang kiat khiam-sun3, un-jiu5 e5 ke2-chi2, chiu7-si7 goa2 e5 cha- bou2-kiaN2 e5 thau5-ke-niu5 koh lai5 goan2 tau bai7 i. Goa2 siuN7 goa2 tioh8 ka7 i he7 cheng5-jit8 sou2 kong2 e5 m7 tioh8, Chong2-si7 goa2 chin m7-ai3, iau2-ku2 goa2 lou7-be2 ka7 i he7. Thau5-ke-niu5 khoaN3-kiN3 goa2 an3- ni, put-chi2 ki5-koai3, khoaN3 i e5 khoan2-sit chhin-chhiuN7 chai i iah8 u7 m7-tioh8. Beh khi3 e5 si5 ka7 goa2 kong2, “Li2 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 na7 beh chhiaN2 toa3 teh ke3 chit8 le2-pai3 iah8 thang.”  
Goa7-kok-lang5 o-lo2 lan2 e5 Tai5-oan5 siu3-le7 ; u7-iaN2,lau2-sit8 bo5 chho3-gou7 . Lan2 Tai5-oan5 e5 soaN kek8 hiong5-chong3 , kek8 u7 pian3-hoa3 , koh-chai3 si7 kek8 chhong-chhui3 , kek8 chheN2-bak8 , si5-siong5 u7 gin5-sek e5 hun5 ,chai7 hiah e5 chheN-leng-leng e5 soaN-chiam e5 tiong-kan teh kun2 teh gia5 . Sou2-i2 che7-chun5 tui3 hai2 tiong-ng hng7-hng7 khoaN3 , tek-khak si7 hui-siong5 sui2 . M7-na7 an2-ni , chiu7-si7 tui3 kin7-kin7 khoaN3 , Tai5-oan5 thau5 kau3 boe2 put-lun7 to2-loh8 chit8 sou2-chai7 , chhiu7-bak8 put-si5 chheN-chhui3 , hoe chhau2 put-si5 iam7-seng7 ; u7 ki5-kha2 e5 boe2-iah8 ti7 hit tiong-kan teh bu2 , ho2-siaN e5 thang5-thoa7 chiau2-chiah teh thi5 teh chhiuN3 , oan2-jian5 si7 chit8-e5 toa7 kong-hng5 . 
Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni lai5 kong2 , chai7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 kin7-kin7 chit8-tiap-a2-ku2 e5 kang-hu , ai3 soaN chiu7 u7 soaN , ai3 hai2 chiu7 u7 hai2 , beh joah8 chiu7 u7 joah8 ,koaN5 chiu7 u7 koaN5 . Sou2-i2 thang kong2 Tai5-oan5 si7 chit8-e5 sio2 Tang-iuN5 . Lan2 Tai5-oan5 u7 chit-khoan2 thian-jian5 e5 ho2-keng2 ,ho2 khi3-hau7 , chiong-lai5 na7-si7 eng7-sim ke lang5 e5 kang-hu toa7-toa7 lai5 cheng2-tun3 , tek-khak e7 chiaN5-cho3 Tang-iuN5 e5 toa7 kong-hng5 , hou7 Tang-iuN5 e5 lang5 chip8-oa2 lai5 hiong2-hok an-lok8 .
